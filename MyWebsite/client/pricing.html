🚀 How It Works: The Process

Getting your business online shouldn't be complicated. I handle the heavy lifting from our first chat to the final launch, keeping you in the loop every step of the way.

1

Discovery & Consultation

We start with a quick conversation to understand your business, your goals, and your preferred design style. We'll outline exactly what pages and features your website needs to succeed.

2

Domain Setup & Design Planning

Once we agree on the direction, I will help you find and purchase the perfect domain name for your brand. Then, I’ll map out a modern, user-friendly design tailored to your specific audience.

3

Development & Admin Integration

This is where the coding happens. I will build your fast, mobile-responsive frontend while simultaneously setting up your secure, easy-to-use backend Admin Panel so you have full control over your content.

4

Review, Polish & Launch

Before going live, I will share a private link for you to review the site. Once you are 100% happy with it, I handle the technical deployment, secure the site with an SSL certificate (HTTPS), and officially launch your new web platform to the world.

5

Handover & Support

After launch, I will show you exactly how to use your new Admin Panel to update text and images. If you’ve opted for premium add-ons like ongoing maintenance or analytics, we'll get those running smoothly as well.
